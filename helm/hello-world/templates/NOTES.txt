Hello World has been deployed! 🎉

To access the service locally, run:

  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "hello-world.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl port-forward $POD_NAME 8080:{{ .Values.service.targetPort }}

Then in another terminal:

  curl http://localhost:8080/
  curl http://localhost:8080/health
  curl http://localhost:8080/metrics

  kubectl get svc {{ include "hello-world.fullname" . }}-lb
